What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical Hacking is the process of simulating a real cyber attack against a company or institute with their prior consent, then documenting any found vulnerabilities or bugs and writing a report with suggestions on how the company should make changes to strengthen their security in order to be more prepared for any attack a real hacker might try.

Computer Science is an ever evolving field, there are always people out there trying to find new ways to make computers do things they were not intended to do.
